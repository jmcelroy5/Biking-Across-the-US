Hi there!

My name is Jessica, and I am a native Texan living in the Bay Area. This summer, after years of dreaming and months of scheming, I'm going to embark on the adventure of a lifetime. I will be biking across the country, following the TransAmerica Trail from Virginia to Oregon then heading down the Pacific Coast back to San Francisco.

When I come back from the trip in fall 2014 I'll have yet another adventure in store. I'll be attending Hackbright Academy, an intensive software engineering fellowship for women. This website is a personal project meant to both document my trip and also learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
